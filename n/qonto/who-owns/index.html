Who owns Qonto?

traditional · Paris, FR · Licence pending (partner model today) · custody: Custodial

short answerQonto is not itself a bank. It runs on a partner-bank (BaaS) model, where a licensed sponsor bank holds the deposits behind the app while Qonto builds the product and interface. Qonto's most notable disclosed backers include Valar Ventures, Alven, Tencent and DST Global.

Is Qonto a licensed bank?

No — it runs on a sponsor bank (BaaS model). Licence detail: Payment institution (ACPR); pursuing credit licence.

Is my money safe with Qonto?

Deposits are held by Qonto's sponsor bank, so any insurance flows through that bank rather than Qonto. Check which sponsor holds your balance and whether it is passed through for per-customer coverage.
