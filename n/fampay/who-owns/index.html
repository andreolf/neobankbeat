FamPay profile

Who owns FamPay?

traditional · Bengaluru, IN · Partner-bank model · custody: Custodial

short answerFamPay is not itself a bank. It runs on a partner-bank (BaaS) model, where a licensed sponsor bank holds the deposits behind the app while FamPay builds the product and interface. FamPay's most notable disclosed backers include Elevation Capital, Y Combinator, Peak XV Partners and General Catalyst.

Is FamPay a licensed bank?

No — it runs on a sponsor bank (BaaS model). Licence detail: Partner bank (IDFC FIRST Bank) — co-branded RBI prepaid payment instruments; RuPay/Visa cards.

Is my money safe with FamPay?

Deposits are held by FamPay's sponsor bank, so any insurance flows through that bank rather than FamPay. Check which sponsor holds your balance and whether it is passed through for per-customer coverage.
