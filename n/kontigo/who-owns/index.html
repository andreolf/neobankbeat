Kontigo profile

Who owns Kontigo?

web3-native · — · Partner-bank model · custody: Custodial

short answerKontigo is not itself a bank. It runs on a partner-bank (BaaS) model, where a licensed sponsor bank holds the deposits behind the app while Kontigo builds the product and interface. Kontigo's most notable disclosed backers include DST Global, Y Combinator, Coinbase Ventures and Soma Capital.

Is Kontigo a licensed bank?

No — it runs on a sponsor bank (BaaS model). Licence detail: Self-custodial MPC wallet (USDC); no licence of its own; fiat rails via partners.

Is my money safe with Kontigo?

Deposits are held by Kontigo's sponsor bank, so any insurance flows through that bank rather than Kontigo. Check which sponsor holds your balance and whether it is passed through for per-customer coverage.
