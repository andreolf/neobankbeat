Flex profile

Who owns Flex?

hybrid · San Francisco, US · Partner-bank model · custody: Custodial

short answerFlex is not itself a bank. It runs on a partner-bank (BaaS) model and works with Column, Lead Bank and Thread Bank — the licensed banks that actually hold customer deposits behind the app. Flex's most notable disclosed backers include Halo Fund, Portage Ventures, Crosslink Capital and Wellington Management.

Is Flex a licensed bank?

No — a partner bank (Column, Lead Bank and Thread Bank) holds the money. Licence detail: Partner banks (Column, Lead, Thread).

Where is your money held?

Is my money safe with Flex?

Your money is held by Column, Lead Bank and Thread Bank, so any deposit insurance flows through that sponsor bank — not Flex itself. Check the sponsor's scheme and whether balances are "passed through" for per-customer insurance.
