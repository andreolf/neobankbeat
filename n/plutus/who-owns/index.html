Plutus profile

Who owns Plutus?

hybrid · London, UK · Self-custodial software · custody: Custodial

short answerPlutus does not hold your money at all — it is self-custodial software. You hold your own keys, so nobody, including Plutus, can move or freeze your funds.

Is Plutus a licensed bank?

No — and it custodies no customer funds; you hold your own keys. Licence detail: E-money card via Modulr (FCA EMI; Lithuanian EMI for EEA); PLU rewards self-custodial.

Where is your money held?

Custody model: Custodial — you hold your own keys; the company never takes custody.

Is my money safe with Plutus?

Plutus never holds your funds, so no deposit-insurance scheme is involved. Your security depends entirely on how you protect your own private keys and recovery phrase — lose them and no company can recover the money for you.
