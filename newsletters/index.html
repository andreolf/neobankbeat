Neobank & fintech newsletters

The inbox stack we actually read to keep this site accurate. Every pick is independent — nobody paid to be here, and there are no affiliate links. Ours is first because it’s ours; the rest are ordered roughly by how often they explain something before anyone else does. Missing a great one? Suggest it.
