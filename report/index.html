why this report exists

Most industry reports count announcements. This one counts survivors: the neobankbeat dataset is hand-verified, survivorship-filtered and open (MIT), so the report's core numbers — 26% licensed banks, 2.7% traditional-wave stablecoin adoption, 30% of the 2020s cohort self-custodial — can be checked by anyone with data.json and ten lines of code. July is edition № 01; every subsequent month reports the same baseline metrics with deltas, building a longitudinal record of the industry.
