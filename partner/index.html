partner with neobankbeat

The open database of 368 neobanks, 106 infrastructure providers and a live board of 3,500+ jobs — read by founders, operators, investors and the AI agents they ask. Independent, no affiliate links, data stays open and free.

The directory, the data and the rankings are not for sale — that's the whole point of the site. What is available: clearly-labeled placement and attention around them.

you run a neobank

Claim and enrich your profile, correct the record, or feature your company in a labeled spotlight slot on the directory and in the newsletter.

you're hiring

Your roles are probably already on the jobs board — pulled from your own careers API. Pin them to the top with a featured highlight, or get your company card promoted.

you sell infra to neobanks

BaaS, issuing, ledgers, compliance — this audience is your exact pipeline. Sponsor the newsletter, the ecosystem map, or a labeled slot on the infra section.

you're an investor or analyst

Custom data cuts, licence and custody breakdowns, market scans and diligence support built on the full dataset — beyond what's in the free data.json.

you're a developer

The dataset is MIT and the API is free — build away, no permission needed. Building something commercial on top and want support or higher-touch data? Say hi.

something else

Press, research collaborations, speaking, or an idea that doesn't fit a box. The form works for that too.

Opens your mail client, addressed to hello@neobankbeat.com — or just email directly. Data corrections are always free: suggest a fix on GitHub.

what stays free, always

Being listed in the directory. Data corrections. The full dataset (data.json, MIT). The API. Appearing on the jobs board — roles are pulled from official career APIs automatically. Sponsored placements are always labeled as such and never change the data, the rankings or who gets tracked.

neobankbeat is independent and open-source. If a partnership would compromise that, it's a no.
