how this board works

neobankbeat tracks 368 verified-active neobanks. For every one that exposes a public careers API (Greenhouse, Lever or Ashby), this board pulls the live postings, classifies them by department and region, and links you straight to the official application page — the same aggregator model as web3.career, but for digital banking. No accounts, no reposts, no fees. A company missing? submit it here — drop the careers-page link and we'll wire it in. Hiring and want your roles seen first? Feature them.

Listings belong to the respective companies and change constantly; this board refreshes on regeneration (last: 2026-07-23). Subscribe to the newest roles via RSS or pull the raw list from jobs/data.json. neobankbeat is independent and earns nothing from applications.
